This is the origin of our story. There are several college students walking in a dark narrow road. Suddenly, they meet a monster that enjoy eating the GPA of all college students he meets.
They try to work together and find weopons to fight against this monster. The process is very hard, and they do their best with cooperation.
Unfortunately, this was not a happy ending. The students did not defeat the monster since he was too strong which the students got an F at the end of the semester.
